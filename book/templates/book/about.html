The funders

Doctor Ava Dom graduated from the University of DOMMDONN in 1989 with a Master of Veterinary Science and located extensively throughout the USA before moving down to New York in 1995. In 2000, after a further five-year study, Ava achieved RKVS board certification in animal internal medicine and is currently the only veterinarian in the surrounding area to have this qualification. Ava also has a degree in Animal Behaviour and is a government-approved Local Vet. Between 2000 and 2007 Jon worked in a bag companion Vet hospital, seeing both first opinion and a variety of referral cases from the University.

Doctor Jack Dom In 1992, after a further four years of study, John achieved RKK board certification in animal internal medicine and is currently the only veterinarian in the surrounding area to have this qualification. Jack also has a degree in Animal therapy and is a government-approved Local Vet. Between 2005 and 2010 Jon worked in a VetRoad hospital, seeing both first opinions and a variety of referral cases from the University.

Doctor Annalise Dom In 1983, after a further seven years of study, Annalise achieved DRD board certification in house animals internal medicine and is currently the only veterinarian in the surrounding area to have this qualification. Annalise also has a degree in heart operation for house animals and is a government approved International Vet. Between 1995 and 2008 Annalise was the CEO of an international vets clinic, seeing both first opinion and a variety of referral cases from international Universities.
